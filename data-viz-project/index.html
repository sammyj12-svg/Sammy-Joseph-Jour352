Concussions in the NFL

How concussion totals have changed over time — and where they are happening the most

By Sammy Joseph

November 17, 2025

The NFL published a report spanning the last 10 years. Showing the amount of concussions per year in the NFL, including at which point in the season they occured. Charts show where most concussions are occuring as well as their frequency in each year.

NFL injury data compiled and analyzed by IQVIA, shows an overall decrease in concussions from 2015-2024. The league’s ongoing 2025 season is excluded due to partial data.

This chart shows how concussions were distributed across different parts of the NFL season from 2015 to 2025. Most concussions occurred during regular-season games, which accounted for 1,476 of the 2,264 total injuries or 65.2%, far exceeding those recorded in the other categories.
